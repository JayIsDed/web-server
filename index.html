About Me

In a world that is ever evolving, you never truly know how anything works. Whether it be the fridge that sits in your kitchen and keeps your food cold, or the device in which you're viewing this website on. You may know how it works fundamentally but there's always more to learn about the resources and tools that we take for granted everyday. This is why it's my mission to hack, tinker, and sometimes break everything in an attempt to find out more about the devices and tools that we use everyday, so that I can repair, improve, and educate myself and others to spread knowledge about the devices and tools that we interface with everyday.
